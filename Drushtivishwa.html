Overview

Drushtivishwa is an inclusive education and support initiative by Dnyansangam Foundation, dedicated to empowering visually impaired students and fostering an inclusive society. Many specially-abled students, especially those with visual impairments, face significant challenges in accessing educational resources, finding writers for exams, and participating in social and career-building activities. These obstacles often lead to limited academic and professional opportunities, affecting their confidence and future prospects.

Drushtivishwa addresses these challenges by providing writer assistance, skill development programs, accessibility resources, and confidence-building events. The initiative not only helps visually impaired students successfully complete their education but also works toward changing societal perceptions and promoting a culture of inclusivity and equal opportunities.

Objectives

  • Providing writer assistance to visually impaired students for exams, helping them pursue their education without limitations.
  • Ensuring access to educational materials such as audiobooks, digital resources, and Braille materials.
  • Building confidence and social inclusion through events, competitions, and interactive programs.
  • Offering skill development training to prepare visually impaired students for career opportunities.
  • Raising awareness in society about the importance of inclusivity and breaking stereotypes regarding disability.

Key Activities

1. Writer Assistance Program

One of the biggest challenges for visually impaired students is the lack of writer support during exams. Drushtivishwa addresses this by:

  • Connecting volunteer writers with visually impaired students.
  • Organizing training sessions for volunteers to assist students effectively.
  • Advocating for long-term solutions and policy changes.

2. Skill Development & Career Readiness

Beyond education, visually impaired students face challenges in employment due to limited access to skill-building opportunities. Drushtivishwa helps them:

  • Gain digital literacy, enabling them to use assistive technology for learning.
  • Develop communication and leadership skills.
  • Explore career opportunities and financial independence.

3. Inclusion Events & Talent Showcases

To encourage participation and self-expression, Drushtivishwa organizes:

  • Cultural events showcasing talents in music, storytelling, and poetry.
  • Confidence-building competitions to improve stage presence.
  • Networking events connecting students with professionals and educators.

4. Awareness & Advocacy Programs

Drushtivishwa works on:

  • Public awareness campaigns to educate about inclusivity.
  • Workshops in schools and colleges to promote disability advocacy.
  • Collaboration with institutions to improve accessibility.
