Overview

Kaathiki is an initiative by Dnyansangam Foundation that aims to discover, nurture, and celebrate storytelling and artistic expression. Many students and young individuals, especially from underprivileged backgrounds, have immense creative potential but lack opportunities to explore fields like storytelling, poetry, music, theater, dance, and literature. Due to financial and social barriers, their talents often go unnoticed, leaving them without a platform to express themselves.

Kaathiki provides that missing stage, offering a safe space for self-expression through storytelling circles, open mic events, cultural performances, and creative workshops. Through monthly events, mentorship programs, and training sessions, this initiative empowers individuals to find their voice, share their narratives, and belong to the world of stories.

Objectives

  • Discover hidden talent in storytelling, performing arts, and creative expression.
  • Provide a platform for individuals to share their stories, poems, songs, and performances.
  • Build confidence in students and young artists by encouraging stage presence.
  • Preserve and promote culture through folk storytelling, traditional art forms, and literature.
  • Create an inclusive creative space where people from different backgrounds can come together and express themselves.

Key Activities

1. Monthly Open Mic & Storytelling Events

Organizing open mic nights where participants can share their stories, poetry, music, and performances. Encouraging students and community members to speak, listen, and connect through stories. Hosting theme-based storytelling sessions where participants share personal narratives or folklore.

2. Workshops & Training Sessions

Conducting storytelling workshops to teach the art of narration, voice modulation, and emotional engagement. Organizing poetry and creative writing sessions to help young writers refine their skills. Providing performance training in theater, dance, and music, with expert guidance.

3. Cultural & Literary Celebrations

Honoring local and folk storytelling traditions, ensuring cultural heritage is preserved. Hosting literature festivals where students and emerging writers can showcase their works. Collaborating with renowned artists, writers, and performers for guest sessions and mentorship.

4. Confidence Building & Public Speaking

Helping participants overcome stage fear and develop public speaking skills. Encouraging self-expression, creativity, and leadership through interactive performances. Creating peer support groups where students can collaborate and refine their artistic skills together.
