About Us

About....

Dnyansangam Foundation, established in 2022 in Pune, is a student-led initiative focused on education, mental well-being, and inclusivity. It aims to bridge learning gaps for underprivileged children by providing quality education, mentorship, and skill development. The foundation also emphasizes mental health by offering counseling, stress management, and community engagement activities. Additionally, it supports specially-abled individuals, helping them integrate into mainstream education and society with dignity. Committed to continuous growth, Dnyansangam works to enhance its educational programs and psychological support services, ensuring that every child has the opportunity to thrive.
